FAQs

When should I start applications?

Ideal runway is 9–12 months before intake for maximum scholarship and housing leverage. We also manage compressed timelines pragmatically.

Do you guarantee admission?

No ethical consultancy guarantees admission. We maximize probability by aligning profile positioning, timing and documentation quality.

How are your services priced?

Modular tiering based on scope: advisory only, application bundle, premium full-stack (visa + post-arrival). Transparent written proposal after diagnostic.

Can you assist with internships abroad?

We provide internship readiness frameworks, CV optimization and LinkedIn positioning but do not fabricate or place directly.
