About Us

Afly Consultancy Services is a British Council certified overseas education consultancy, dedicated to guiding students to top universities worldwide. Our expert counselors provide personalized support, from university selection to visa guidance and post-arrival services.

  • Certified, ethical, and transparent counseling

  • Instant WhatsApp and consultation support

Why Choose Us?

We believe in genuine, student-first guidance. Our British Council certification and direct university partnerships ensure you get the best advice and opportunities for your global education journey.
