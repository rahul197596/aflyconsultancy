About Afly Consultancy

Afly Consultancy Services is dedicated to empowering students to achieve their global education dreams. With a team of experienced counselors and a network of top universities worldwide, we provide personalized guidance, expert visa support, and access to exclusive scholarships.

Our mission is to make overseas education accessible, seamless, and rewarding for every student. We believe in a student-first approach, offering honest advice, transparent processes, and end-to-end support from application to arrival.
