About Us

Afly Consultancy Services is a British Council certified overseas education consultancy, dedicated to guiding students to top universities worldwide. Our expert counselors provide personalized support — from university selection and applications to visa strategy and post-arrival enablement.

  • 900+ university touchpoints across the UK, USA, Australia, Canada, New Zealand, and Europe
  • Certified, ethical, and transparent counseling
  • End-to-end support: admissions, scholarships, loans, pre-departure, and more
  • Responsive WhatsApp and consultation support

Why Choose Us?

We believe in genuine, student-first guidance. Our British Council certification and direct university partnerships ensure you get evidence-based advice and the right opportunities for your global education journey.
